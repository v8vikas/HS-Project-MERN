What can I use Otter for?

Otter turns your voice conversations into smart notes that you can easily search and share. You can use it to take notes at your meetings and interviews, capture your thoughts and ideas while you’re driving in the car, and transcribe your existing recordings and podcasts. You can even snap photos (e.g. of a whiteboard, or a speaker or presentation slide at an event) during a recording and they will be inserted inline with your transcripts. The possibilities are endless.

Is my data safe?

Yes, we are committed to keeping your data private and secure. We do not sell or share your data with anybody (except as necessary to respond to lawful requests, etc). We may select segments of raw or partially processed audio and associated transcribed text from multiple recorded conversations from different users to be used as training data to automatically train our proprietary artificial intelligence (AI) technology. Only after receiving explicit permission from our users, we may manually transcribe certain recorded conversations to further enrich our training data. We sync your data over an encrypted connection, and store it in our secure data center that has both physical and electronic security.

FAQ Section Style 03
Q.
What is Bigcommerce?
A.

Bigcommerce is the #1 Ecommerce software solution platform that allows you to create an online business website.

Q.
What is involved in the installation of a template or theme inside of my Bigcommerce store?
A.

There are changes you can make within a Bigcommerce template or theme, but since Bigcommerce is a hosted shopping cart platform, the theme is already installed. To have the theme look its best in your store, it may need some minor adjustments. Installing a template includes manually updating the exact settings you want and testing your store front’s functionality.

Q.
Can Bigcommerce support custom programming?
A.

Yes. Bigcommerce partners with different experts who are able to do custom programming on a Bigcommerce site. Coalition Technologies is an expert when it comes to customizing a Bigcommerce site. Coalition specializes in modifications to Bigcommerce templates. Coalition is able to develop the Bigcommerce platform in a way that best fits your business.

Q.
Can I purchase the template and install them myself?
A.

The majority of the templates in the Bigcommerce catalog are built with html and css coding, not specific to Bigcommerce, so purchasing the templates is not allowed at this time.
